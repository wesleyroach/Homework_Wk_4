Crystal Collector

Instructions

You will be assigned a random number to start the game. There are 4 crystals you can see below. By clicking on a crystal you will add an amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose if your total score goes above that random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from view until you click on it. When the game starts the values will be changed for each crystal.
